The Future of AI

The Future of AI looks to be very promising as it continues to grow. The possibilites are essentially endless. Look below to see further on how AI can flourish into a tool that will help everyone, always.

Why is AI important?

Every year AI is starting to integrate into our every day life a bit more. With this advancement, humans are able to rely on AI's to complete menial or surface level tasks and are then able to focus on the deeper issues and problems that affect us. Through this method we will be able to solve problems at a never before seen pace. We will see a massive growth in our society and quality of lives.

Possible Future Applications of Arificial Intelligence:

  • Quantum Computing
  • Automated Construction
  • Neurotechnology
  • Technical Advancements
  • Cyber Security
  • Automated Agriculture
  • Space/Deep Space Exploration
  • Climate Change Mitigation

The potential of Apps for Artifical Intelligence is endless. AI could replace a huge chunk of work in the future.

A New Type of AI Model to Emerge.

In the future, it is theorized that AI models may be able to gain sentient consciousness, meaning they would be as aware of themselves as humans are of themselves. This breakthrough may completely change how we see AI's and may change the future of society forever. If they were to gain this advancement they would also gain the ability to have free will. This may change our view of robots and AI's to a more equal standpoint with humans, instead of just servants.
